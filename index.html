Crystal Collector

How to play


  1. You will start with a random number when the game begins.
  2. There are four crystals to choose from.
  3. Each crystal hold a hidden specific amount of points. Click them to reach total score
  4. You win by reaching to the random number that is the total score
  5. You lose by going over that number!
  6. Everytime the game restarts the value to each crystal randomizes
